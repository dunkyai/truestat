## Key Findings This Week

### 1. Louisiana's Health Science Pipeline
Louisiana averages a poor 33.2 ROI, but its health-focused institutions dramatically outperform: LSU Health Sciences (65), Baton Rouge General Nursing (64), ITI Technical (62), and Tulane (61). Health science programs are carrying the state.
→ Content issue created: TRU-6

### 2. Maine Maritime Academy — The Hidden Public Gem
At $23K/yr in-state, this public school produces $90K earners (ROI 71). Beats most New England private colleges on earnings at a fraction of the cost. Maritime academies are systematically underrated.
→ Content issue created: TRU-7

### 3. Ultra-Cheap ≠ Good ROI
Henry Ford College costs just $660/yr but has ROI of 26 with $35K 10yr earnings. Several sub-$10K schools show that low price doesn't guarantee value when lifetime earnings stay flat.
→ Content issue created: TRU-8
